About Our Mission

Dedicated to environmental data transparency

Monitoring Water Quality Since 2000

Our organization has been at the forefront of water quality monitoring for over two decades. We've expanded from 120 basins in 2000 to 144 basins in 2021, providing comprehensive coverage of critical waterways.

Through rigorous data collection and analysis, we've helped identify pollution trends and supported environmental policy decisions that have led to significant improvements in water quality indicators.
